Privacy Policy

Summary: When you request a quote or submit a form, we collect the information you provide (like contact details and project info) so we can respond and connect you with solar professionals who can provide estimates.

Information we collect

  • Contact info (name, phone, email).
  • Location info (ZIP / city / county) and basic property details you share.
  • Project info (utility provider, monthly bill, battery interest, notes).
  • Technical info (basic analytics like pages visited, UTM parameters) if enabled.

How we use information

  • To respond to your request and communicate about solar quotes.
  • To match your request with one or more installation partners that serve your area.
  • To improve our site, forms, and lead quality.
  • To prevent fraud/spam and protect our systems.

Sharing your information

To help you get quotes, we may share your submitted information with third-party solar installation partners and service providers (for example, CRM, email, analytics). Those partners may contact you by phone, text, or email about your request.

Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information by emailing privacy@mountaineersolar.com.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications by following unsubscribe instructions (if provided) or contacting us.

Data retention

We keep lead submissions as long as needed to fulfill your request, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

This site is not intended for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time and will revise the “Last updated” date below.
